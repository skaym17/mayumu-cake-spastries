Our Story

At Mayumu Cakes & Pastries, our journey began with a simple dream and a sprinkle of passion. It all started when Michelle D. Miranda fueled by an unwavering love for baking, decided to turn her delectable creations into something more. With a whisk in hand and determination in her heart, she embarked on a mission to create a cake shop that would leave taste buds dancing and hearts singing. Building a business from scratch wasn't a piece of cake. We faced numerous challenges along the way—hours spent perfecting recipes, countless late nights baking, and navigating the intricacies of entrepreneurship. But our shared vision kept us motivated, and our love for creating mouthwatering delights fueled our determination

As we continue to grow, we remain committed to pushing boundaries, experimenting with flavors, and delivering moments of pure bliss. We invite you to join us on this sweet adventure and indulge in the magic that unfolds when passion and pastry come together. Together, let's create memories that are as delightful as the cakes we create.
